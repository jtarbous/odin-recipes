Pork Belly Ramen

This is a homemade recipe for pork belly ramen! Total cook time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • One (1) Pork Ramen from POM
  • Ten (10) pieces of thiny sliced pork belly
  • One quarter (1/4) cup diced scallions
  • One half (1/2) cup of baby spinach
  • Two (2) eggs
  • Hot oil to taste of spiciness

Directions

  1. Boil two (2) cups of water
  2. Brown pork belly on skillet
  3. Add noodles to boiling water, wait 5 minutes
  4. Combine pork belly, scallions, and baby spinach into pot
  5. Add both eggs, let cook 3 minutes
  6. Add powders/seasoining to pot, stir, and enjoy!
